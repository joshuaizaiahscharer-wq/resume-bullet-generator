    Yes. The AI uses industry-specific keywords and strong action verbs — two of the biggest factors in passing applicant tracking systems.

    Can I use the bullets exactly as generated?

    Absolutely, but we recommend personalizing them with real numbers and specifics from your own experience. Recruiters love specificity.

    What job titles does it support?

    Any job title — from entry-level roles like Cashier or Barista to senior positions like VP of Engineering or Chief Marketing Officer. If the job exists, BulletAI can write bullets for it.

    How is this different from ChatGPT?

    BulletAI uses a specialized prompt fine-tuned specifically for resume bullet generation. You get structured, ready-to-use output without crafting a prompt yourself.
